Chang Liu

Hi there, I'm Chang Liu

I am a Ph.D. student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at University of California, Davis. My advisor is Prof. Chen-Nee Chuah. Prior to joining UC Davis, I received my B.S. from Chu Kochen Honors College, Zhejiang University. I am proud to be a member of ACEE (Advanced Honor Class of Engineering Education (60/5000+), class 2008).

My research interests are software-defined networking, network monitoring and network security.
